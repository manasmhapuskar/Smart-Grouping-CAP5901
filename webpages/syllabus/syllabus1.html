Course Description

This Applied Business Statistics course is about learning how we can calculate various forms of statistics

in the field of Businesses. A Business expert needs to be able to communicate effectively with clients

and stakeholders; to generate, evaluate, critique, explain and justify their business ideas by referencing

their calcuated statistics; to meet the client requiremnts; and to make tradeoffs between some negotiations

in order to get a better deal.
